About SOMDA

SOMDA was founded with a singular mission: to provide unwavering legal advocacy and support for families facing the devastating impact of gender-based violence and family conflict. Our organization emerged from the recognition that vulnerable populations often lack access to quality legal representation and comprehensive support services. Since our establishment, we have grown into a trusted beacon of hope for hundreds of families across the region, combining legal expertise with compassionate care to deliver justice and protection.

At the heart of SOMDA lies a commitment to our core values: justice, integrity, compassion, and empowerment. We believe every person deserves to live free from violence and fear, with their rights protected and their voice heard. Our team of dedicated professionals—attorneys, investigators, counselors, and advocates—work collaboratively to address not just the legal dimensions of family conflict, but the emotional and practical needs of our clients. We are driven by the belief that true change comes through persistent advocacy, systemic reform, and unwavering support for those we serve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and process

SOMDA specializes in a comprehensive range of family law and gender-based violence cases, including domestic violence protection orders, custody and guardianship disputes, divorce and separation proceedings, property division, maintenance claims, sexual assault cases, and harassment matters. Our experienced team also handles cases involving child protection, inheritance disputes within families, and mediation for family conflicts. If you're unsure whether your case falls within our scope, we encourage you to contact us for a confidential consultation.

Confidentiality is paramount to our practice. All communications between you and our attorneys are protected by attorney-client privilege. Our investigators maintain strict confidentiality protocols, and all case files are securely stored with limited access. We comply with all applicable privacy laws and professional ethical standards. Your safety and privacy are our top priorities, and we take extraordinary measures to protect your personal information and case details from unauthorized disclosure.

Our fee structure is transparent and flexible, designed to accommodate various financial situations. We offer hourly rates, fixed fees for specific services, and contingency arrangements in appropriate cases. We also work with clients on payment plans and can discuss pro bono or reduced-fee services for qualifying individuals. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed fee estimate and discuss all available payment options. We believe cost should never prevent someone from accessing quality legal representation.

The timeline for legal proceedings varies significantly depending on the type of case, its complexity, court schedules, and whether the matter is contested or uncontested. Some cases may be resolved within weeks through negotiation or mediation, while others may take several months or years if they proceed to trial. Protection orders can often be obtained quickly, sometimes within days. During your initial consultation, we'll provide a realistic timeline estimate based on your specific circumstances and answer any questions about what to expect throughout the process.

SOMDA recognizes that legal issues are often accompanied by emotional, psychological, and practical challenges. We provide comprehensive support including referrals to qualified trauma-informed counselors and therapists, connections to support groups for survivors, assistance with safe housing and relocation planning, information about financial assistance programs, and guidance on accessing victim services. Our holistic approach ensures you receive the complete support needed to heal and move forward, not just legal solutions to your immediate problems.
